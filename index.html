All about Golden Retrievers!

Golden Retrievers are very friendly and are a great pet to have!

The lifespan of a golden retriever is from 10-12 years.

Their temperament is reliable, friendly, kind, and confident.

The average wieght of females is from 55–71 lbs (25–32 kg).

The average wieght of males is from 65–75 lbs (30–34 kg).

The average height of females is from 20–22 inches (51–56 cm)

The average height of males is from 22–24 inches (56–61 cm)

Golden Retrievers can come in Light Golden, Golden, Dark Golden, and Cream

All about Labrador Retrievers!

The Labrador Retriever, also Labrador, is a type of retriever-gun dog. It is also very fun to play with and useful!

The lifespan of a labrador retriever is from 10-12 years.

Their temperament is Outgoing, Even Tempered, Gentle, Agile, Kind, Intelligent, and Trusting.

The average wieght of females is from 55–71 lbs (25–32 kg).

The average wieght of males is from Male: 64–79 lbs (29–36 kg).

The average height of females is from 20–22 inches (51–56 cm)

The average height of males is from 22–24 inches (56–61 cm)

Labrador Retrievers can come in Black, Yellow, Chocolate

All about Australian Shepards!

The Australian Shepherd is a medium size breed of dog that was developed on ranches in the western United States!

The lifespan of a labrador retriever is from 13-15 years.

Their temperament is Protective, Good-natured, Active, Intelligent, Affectionate.

The average height of females is from 18–21 inches (46–54 cm).

The average height of males is from 20–23 inches (51–58 cm).

The average weight of males is from 55–70 lbs (25–32 kg)

The average weight of females is from 35–55 lbs (16–25 kg)

Australian Shepards can come in Black, Red Merle, Red tricolor, Merle, Red, Black tricolor, Blue Merle

All about Bernese Mountain Dogs!

The Bernese Mountain Dog called in German the Berner Sennenhund, is a large-sized breed of dog, one of the four breeds of Sennenhund-type dogs from the Swiss Alps.

The lifespan of a Bernese Mountain Dog is from 6-8 years.

Their temperament is Loyal, Faithful, Intelligent, Affectionate.

The average height of females is from 23–26 inches (58–66 cm).

The average height of males is from 25–28 inches (64–70 cm).

The average weight of males is from 86–110 lbs (39–50 kg)

The average weight of females is from 79–110 lbs (36–48 kg)

Bernese Mountain Dog can come in Tricolor (black, rust, and white)
